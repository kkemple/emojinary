Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I get token mismatch error in Slack

This is mostly caused by a missing or bad token saved for your settings. Go to setup and follow the instructions for setting up your Slack slash command.

I want to upgrade, how do I give you all my :money_with_wings:

I want to cancel my Pro account, how do I stop giving you all of my :money_with_wings:

I really hate to see you go, but if you must move on who am I to stop you (just the guy who will starve to death and die homeless without your generous contributions :wink:). You can stop giving your :moneybag: to me by going to settings and clicking "Cancel Pro".
